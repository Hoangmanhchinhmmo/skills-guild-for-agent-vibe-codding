## Enriched Skills

### `saas-mvp-launcher`
**What you need:** Walks you through planning and building the first version of a software-as-a-service product quickly, from picking the right tech stack to getting your first paying users.
**Level:** Intermediate
**Tags:** #saas #mvp #startup #product-launch #full-stack
**When to use:**
- You have a SaaS idea and want to go from zero to a working product as fast as possible
- You need help choosing the right tech stack, database, and hosting for a new SaaS
- You want a step-by-step plan that covers auth, billing, dashboards, and deployment
**Combine with:** `saas-mvp-launcher` > `stripe-integration` > `landing-page-generator` > `launch-strategy`
**Example prompt:** > /saas-mvp-launcher Plan and build an MVP for a SaaS that helps freelancers track invoices and send payment reminders to clients
**Don't use when:** You are building a tiny single-purpose tool rather than a subscription service -- use `personal-tool-builder` instead

### `stripe-integration`
**What you need:** Helps you add payment processing to your app using Stripe, covering everything from accepting credit cards to setting up subscriptions, webhooks, and checkout flows.
**Level:** Intermediate
**Tags:** #stripe #payments #billing #subscriptions #checkout #e-commerce
**When to use:**
- You need to accept one-time or recurring payments in your web app
- You want to set up Stripe Checkout, subscription billing, or a customer portal
- You need to handle Stripe webhooks to keep your app in sync with payment events
**Combine with:** `saas-mvp-launcher` > `stripe-integration` > `clerk-auth` > `firebase`
**Example prompt:** > /stripe-integration Add Stripe subscription billing to my Next.js app with three pricing tiers, a checkout page, and webhook handling for payment events
**Don't use when:** You need to automate Stripe operations at scale rather than build an integration -- use `stripe-automation` instead

### `shopify-development`
**What you need:** Guides you through building on the Shopify platform, including creating custom themes, building Shopify apps, and setting up online stores with advanced features.
**Level:** Intermediate
**Tags:** #shopify #e-commerce #themes #apps #liquid #storefront
**When to use:**
- You want to build a custom Shopify theme or modify an existing one
- You need to create a Shopify app that adds features to a merchant's store
- You are setting up a Shopify store and need help with Liquid templates, product pages, or checkout customization
**Combine with:** `shopify-development` > `stripe-integration` > `seo-fundamentals` > `landing-page-generator`
**Example prompt:** > /shopify-development Build a custom Shopify theme section that displays a filterable product grid with color swatches and quick-add-to-cart buttons
**Don't use when:** You just need to automate store operations like inventory updates or order management -- use `shopify-automation` instead
