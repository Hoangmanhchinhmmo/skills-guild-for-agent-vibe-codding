## Day la gi?

**Vibe coding** = xay dung phan mem voi AI, ngay ca khi ban khong phai developer truyen thong. Claude Code co **1,282+ skills** — kha nang chuyen biet bien no thanh chuyen gia cho bat ky nhiem vu nao.

Repo nay to chuc moi skill vao **21 danh muc**, cho ban biet **moi skill lam gi** bang tieng Viet de hieu, va chi cho ban **ket hop skills nao** cho du an thuc te.

Du ban la founder dang xay MVP, designer muon code, hay developer kham pha AI — bat dau tu day.

---

## Bat dau nhanh

```
1. Duyet cac danh muc ben duoi hoac dung Bang tim skill
2. Chon 1 skill (hoac combo cho du an day du)
3. Dung /ten-skill trong Claude Code
```

Vay thoi. Khong can setup, khong can cai dat, khong can config.

## Phan loai Skill

Moi skill co tag muc do de giup ban quyet dinh:

| Tag | Y nghia | Danh cho ai |
|-----|---------|------------|
| **[CORE]** | Skill thiet yeu, dung rong rai | Tat ca moi nguoi |
